Student Life

Collaboration, support, and remote pair programming are at the center of your Microverse experience as you learn through building real projects.

Your Coding Partner & Stand-Up Team Learning to code on your own can be lonely and difficult. At Microverse, you’ll join one of the most collaborative and social online school in the world.

Every day, you, your coding partner, and your stand-up team will support and hold each other accountable to produce great work while remote pair programming.

Your Dedicated Mentors & Career Coaches In addition to your coding partner and stand-up team, you’ll also get mentors and a career coach to support you in your journey at Microverse.

From reviewing your code and answering tough questions to helping you negotiate job offers, you’ll be in good company as you go through the program. Learn more about the support you will receive ➞

Your Daily Schedule Microverse’s immersive, full-time structure assures that you continuously push yourself to your full potential.

Every day, you’ll complete a coding challenge with your stand-up team and then continue learning with your coding partner through remote pair programming while developing valuable skills that employers look for in candidates. Learn more about your daily schedule at Microverse ➞
